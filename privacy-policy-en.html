Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how No Te Pases ("we", "our app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: By using our application, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Location Data

Our application requires access to your location to provide its main functionalities:

  • Real-time location: We collect your current GPS location to show your position on the map and calculate the distance to your destination.
  • Location history: We store locally on your device the following information about destinations you save:
    • Destination name
    • Geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude)
    • Physical address
    • Creation date
    • Notes or favorite markers

1.2. Usage Data

To improve our services and show relevant advertising, we may collect:

  • Information about how you use the application
  • Data on interaction with advertisements
  • Device information (type, operating system, device identifiers)

1.3. Permissions Requested

Our application requests the following permissions:

  • Precise location (GPS): Necessary to show your location on the map and perform route tracking.
  • Approximate location: Alternative when precise location is not available.
  • Internet: Necessary to load maps and display advertising.
  • Network state: To verify connectivity.
  • Notifications: To show notifications about distance to destination during tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. Application Features

  • Show your current location on the map
  • Calculate and display the distance to your destination
  • Send notifications when you approach your destination
  • Save and manage your favorite and recent destinations
  • Provide alarms when you reach the programmed distance

2.2. Service Improvement

We use aggregated and anonymous data to:

  • Improve application functionality
  • Fix errors and technical problems
  • Analyze usage patterns

2.3. Advertising

We display ads in our application through third-party advertising networks. These ads may be based on:

  • Your general location (city/region, not exact location)
  • Device information
  • Application usage data

3. Data Storage

3.1. Local Storage

All your personal data (destinations, favorites, history) is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Local SQLite database: To store your destinations, coordinates, addresses, and notes.
  • SharedPreferences: To store temporary tracking settings.

We do not send your personal data to our servers. Your destinations and locations remain on your device.

3.2. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete it from the application
  • You uninstall the application (in which case all local data will be deleted)

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

4.1. Third-Party Services Used

Our application uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Maps API

  • Purpose: Display maps and geocode addresses
  • Data shared: Address queries and coordinates to display maps
  • Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Appodeal (Advertising Network)

  • Purpose: Display advertisements in the application
  • Data shared: Device identifiers, general location, usage data for ad personalization
  • Privacy policy: https://appodeal.com/privacy-policy

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Serve ads through the Appodeal network
  • Data shared: Device identifiers, usage data, general location
  • Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We only share information with the third-party services mentioned above, which are necessary for the application to function.

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted local storage when possible
  • Restricted access to location data only when the application is in use
  • We do not transmit exact location data to external servers

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Although we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can access all your saved data directly from the application
  • Deletion: You can delete any destination or saved data from the application at any time
  • Permission control: You can revoke location permissions from your device settings
  • Uninstallation: When you uninstall the application, all your local data will be automatically deleted

7. Children Under 13

Our application is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information from our systems.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. International Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device. However, the third-party services we use (Google, Appodeal) may process data in different countries. By using our application, you agree that your data may be transferred and processed in these countries.
